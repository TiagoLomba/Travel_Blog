Next trip already booked!

I am so excited to be visiting Miami on February 21st! There are so many things on my must-see list for this trip.

South Beach

One of my top priorities is to visit the famous South Beach. I can't wait to feel the sand between my toes and take a dip in the crystal clear waters. I've heard the sunsets here are absolutely stunning, so I'll make sure to catch one while I'm there.

Little Havana

I'm also really looking forward to experiencing the culture of Little Havana. I've heard that Calle Ocho is the place to be to check out street art, live music and authentic Cuban restaurants. I'm excited to try some of the local food and maybe even pick up some souvenirs to bring back home.

Everglades National Park

I'm also really interested in visiting the Everglades National Park. I've heard that this place is a nature lover's paradise and I can't wait to see alligators, panthers and other native wildlife.

Perez Art Museum Miami and Adrienne Arsht Center

Perez Art Museum Miami and the Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts are also on my list

I'll be sure to share my experiences and photos with you all, so stay tuned!
